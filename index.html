About Me


I am an aspiring web developer from Minnesota. I have been playing board games since the early 2000's. But I became a serious hobby gamer around 2017. I enjoy all sorts of differnt games, from welcoming games to heavy strategy.


I created this site to combine my two passions: board gaming and programming. I play a lot of solo variants of games, so I often find that a digital deck can save a lot of time. Which means I get to play more!
